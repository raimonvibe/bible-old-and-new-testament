# Romans 9 capitalization pilot

Contextual pass applied 2026-07-22 using `docs/capitalization-style-guide.md`.

## Changes (WEB → reverential)

| Verse | Change | Reason |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 15 | he → **He** | God speaking to Moses |
| 16 | Him → **him** (×2) | Human “who wills / who runs”, not God |
| 18 | he → **He** (×3) | God has mercy / hardens |
| 19 | he → **He**, his → **His** | Objection about God’s will |
| 23–24 | he/his → **He/His** (×4) | God prepares vessels, calls believers |
| 25 | he → **He** | God speaking through Hosea |
| 33 | him → **Him** | Stumbling stone = Christ (Isaiah quote) |

## Unchanged (already correct)

- v11: **Him** who calls (God)
- v20: **Him** who formed it (God/potter)
- v22: **His** wrath, **His** power
- v28: **He** will finish (Lord in Isaiah quote)
